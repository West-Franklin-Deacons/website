Service Times and Accessibility

Cultural Entry Points vs. Barriers

Key Cultural Considerations

Current Cultural Expectations

  • Traditional Sunday service timing (potentially excluding shift workers)
  • Assumed familiarity with church culture
  • Expectations about dress and behavior
  • Presumed knowledge of Christian terminology

Opportunities for Greater Accessibility

  • Alternative meeting times and formats
  • Community-based gatherings
  • Flexible entry points for newcomers
  • Intentional cultural bridge-building

Moving Beyond "Club Membership"

  • Rethinking traditional program structures
  • Creating multiple pathways to belong
  • Meeting people where they are
  • Emphasizing relationship over conformity
