Growing Our Impact: A Step-by-Step Approach

Level 1: Simple Sharing (Starting Point)

  • Share church social media posts about events and activities
  • Like and comment on church updates
  • Mention upcoming church events to friends
  • Share photos from church activities you enjoy

Level 2: Personal Invitation

  • Invite friends to social events or holiday services
  • Share what you enjoy about your life group
  • Bring neighbors to community service projects
  • Tell stories about positive church experiences

Level 3: Deeper Connections

  • Share how church has impacted your life personally
  • Listen for and respond to friends' spiritual questions
  • Participate in community service with church groups
  • Share brief testimonials in comfortable settings

Level 4: Faith Conversations

  • Join a mentor pair to learn from experienced members
  • Practice sharing your faith story in life groups
  • Learn simple ways to transition from life to faith discussions
  • Participate in community outreach events

Level 5: Community Impact

  • Lead community service initiatives
  • Mentor others in sharing their faith
  • Develop new outreach opportunities
  • Share your experience with newer members

Supporting Resources for Each Level:

  • Short video examples of natural conversations
  • Shareable social media content templates
  • Simple conversation starter cards
  • Monthly life group discussion guides
  • Regular training opportunities
